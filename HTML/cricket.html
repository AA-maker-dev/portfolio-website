Cricket

  • Overview: A bat-and-ball sport played between two teams of eleven players on a circular field.
  • Objective: Score runs by hitting a bowled ball and dismiss the batting team by getting them out.
  • Game Structure: Matches are divided into innings, with teams alternating between batting and fielding.
  • Formats: Includes Test matches (up to 5 days), One-Day Internationals (ODI), and Twenty20 (T20, shortest format).
  • Global Reach: Highly popular in countries like India, England, Australia, and others, with a massive fan base.
  • Key Elements: Known for strategic depth, skill in batting, bowling, and fielding, and rich traditions.
