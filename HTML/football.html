Football

  • Overview: A globally popular team sport played between two teams of eleven players on a rectangular field with goals at each end.
  • Objective: Score goals by kicking a ball into the opposing team's goal, while defending your own goal.
  • Game Structure: Matches last 90 minutes, divided into two 45-minute halves, with teams alternating attack and defense.
  • Formats: Includes professional leagues, international tournaments like the FIFA World Cup, and amateur or street games.
  • Global Reach: The world's most watched and played sport, with massive followings in Europe, South America, Africa, and beyond.
  • Key Elements: Emphasizes teamwork, strategy, athleticism, and skills like passing, dribbling, and shooting.
